Welcome to my personal webpage.


In short

Since 1.09.2022 I am postdoctoral fellow at the Laboratoire de Mathématiques d’Orsay, UMR CNRS 8628 in université Paris-Saclay, hosted by Nicolas Curien and Nathanaël Enriquez.

From 1.10.2020 to 31.08.2022 I have been a postdoctoral research and teaching assistant (full time ATER) at Laboratoire d’Analyse et de Mathématiques Appliquées, UMR CNRS 8050 in université Paris-Est Créteil, hosted by Arnaud Le Ny.

I defended my PhD thesis on Friday 16.10.2020 at the université Paris-Saclay under the co-direction of William Jalby, Olivier Rivoire and Andrea Sportiello.
During my PhD I have been associated member at Laboratoire d'Informatique de Paris Nord, UMR CNRS 7030 in université Sorbonne Paris Nord.
