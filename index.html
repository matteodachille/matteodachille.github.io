About me

Postdoctoral researcher at the Laboratoire de Mathématiques d’Orsay and member of the ERC Grant "SuPerGRandMa", PI Nicolas Curien. See CV for more.

My research interests lie in probability, mainly in random geometry, statistical mechanics and random combinatorial optimization problems.

Keywords: ideal Poisson-Voronoi tessellations, massive spanning forests, Gibbs measures/specifications, (Euclidean) random assignment problems and extensions. See publications for more and gallery for an introduction by visuals.
