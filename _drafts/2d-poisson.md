Consider the _fundamental problem of electrostatics_ in two dimensions

\\[\nabla_x \cdot \left(\eta(x)\nabla_x G(x,y) \right)=-\delta^{(2)}(x-y)\\]

where \\(\eta(x) = \frac{1}{2\pi}e^{-\frac{x^2}{2}}\\)
